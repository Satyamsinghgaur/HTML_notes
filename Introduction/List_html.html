List Element

There are two type of list in html:-

  1. Unordered List (ol)
  2. Ordered List (ol)

we use List Item (li) for showing the list items.

  • Unordered List :-

    This shows the list where the order of items is not important. In this items are presented in the form of Bullets.

  • Ordered List :-

    This shows the list where the order of items is important. In this items are presented in the form of Numbers (ie: - 2, II, (ii) etc).
